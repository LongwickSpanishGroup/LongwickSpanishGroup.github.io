What's Available to Spanish Learners in Princes Risborough and Surrounding Areas

We're only aiming to list what's available for adult learners.

In-person Learning

  • If you're retired or semi-retired, U3A offers a monthly meeting for those who'd like to learn Spanish. It's free for members and aimed at beginners.
  • Spanish Conversation Group offers weekly meetings at the Red Lion pub in Longwick. They're free, although you're expected to buy a drink from the pub. There's no explicit teaching going on, but it's a good place to practice your spoken Spanish and socialise with like-minded people. Speakers of all levels are welcome.
  • Superprof has a list of Spanish tutors in the Princes Risborough area. The prices vary.

If you know of other resources available, please drop us a line, and we'll add them to this page.
