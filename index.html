Welcome to Longwick Spanish Conversation Group

About Us

Longwick Spanish Conversation Group is a free Spanish club where people meet in informal settings and chat in Spanish.

We meet every Tuesday at the Red Lion pub in Longwick, Buckinghamshire, from 6:30 to 8:00pm.

The pub is located at Thame Rd, Longwick, Princes Risborough HP27 9SG.

We meet in the dining area, and although there's no requirement to buy food, the pub kindly asks that each visitor supports them by purchasing at least one drink.

There's no explicit teaching involved; the idea is to try and speak about the topic we're discussion in Spanish to help unblock your conversational skills.

Participation is free. However, we have a limited number of spaces available, so please don't come unannounced. Instead, please join our Facebook group, find the event for next Tuesday, and select 'Going'.

If you don't have a Facebook profile but still would like to participate, please get in touch with us by email: LongwickSpanishGroup@gmail.com

For more information and to join us, visit our Facebook page.
