Shows and Films in Spanish with Spanish or English Subtitles

14-Mar-2024

A popular question is where one can watch Spanish language shows and films.

The answer is Netflix. It has many shows in Spanish. When you start watching a show, it might just show it to you in English because most of them have English translations, but you can change the audio to Spanish, and you can turn the subtitles on (in Spanish or English, whatever you prefer). You can also change the language to Spanish - Audio Description, which is intended for those who can't see very well. When there are no dialogues, a narrator is describing what is going on. I find it super useful for language learners!

A few cool shows in Spanish on Netflix:

  • Criminal: Spain (by the way, there's also Criminal: UK, Criminal: France, Criminal: Germany, all pretty cool)
  • The Neighbour (El Vecino)
  • Money Heist (La Casa de Papel)
  • The House of Flowers (La Casa de las Flores)
  • High Seas (Alta Mar)
  • Society of the Snow (La sociedad de la nieve)

Those are just some examples, there are tons more shows.
